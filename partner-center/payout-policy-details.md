## <a name="process-for-customer-non-payment"></a><span data-ttu-id="da34b-177">客户非付款过程</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="da34b-177">Process for customer non-payment</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="da34b-178">在极少数情况下，Microsoft 无法收集客户对其商业市场购买的付款。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="da34b-178">On rare occasions, Microsoft is unable to collect payments from customers for their commercial marketplace purchases.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="da34b-179">当客户无法根据其计费计划向 Microsoft 付款时，我们开始进行集合过程。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="da34b-179">When a customer fails to pay Microsoft according to their billing schedule, we begin the collections process.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="da34b-180">此过程大约需要四个月的时间，并涉及与 Microsoft 的持久通信。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="da34b-180">This process takes approximately four months and involves persistent communication from Microsoft.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="da34b-181">如果此过程结束时未收到付款，则 Microsoft 会将资金作为 uncollectable 写出。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="da34b-181">If payment is not received by the end of this process, Microsoft writes off the funds as uncollectable.</span></span>
